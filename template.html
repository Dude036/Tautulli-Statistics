Total Watch Count by Month and Hour

The graph on the left here shows the most common hour of the day is 8 AM, with 9 PM being a very close second. We have morning show people, and evening show people. My initial thought would be a quick pick up, starting around 7 am, a steady rise until midnight, and a sharp decline into the wee hours of the morning.

The graph on the right shows the usage compared by month. The Summer is clearly the time where more people are doing stuff outside, but I also had the server down for about a week while I was moving, so that probably tainted the results a little. And to no ones surprise, December is when everyone is watching Christmas movies and relaxing inside staying cozy and warm.
